Who can register?

Any data scientist, programmer, designer, statistician and researcher - any person who loves data. You can be a student, an industry professional, or someone from academia. We don’t care. It’s best to come with at least some experience in programming, data science or design. However, you do need to be at least 18.

Forming teams

We welcome teams formed outside of the event and those who attend as individuals. The DataHack organizers will help you build a team where your great ideas and love for data shines through. Our registration process incroporates HakkaLabs' matching mechanism to help you find other participants with complementary skill sets to form a team with. Teams of up to five members are allowed.
