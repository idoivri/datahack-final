FAQ

When is DataHack?

This year’s event will take place on November 25th – 27th, 2015. The event will start on Wednesday, November 25th afternoon and run until Friday, November 27th afternoon. A notice will be given on exact times.

Do I need to pay?

No, participating in DataHack is totally free, and includes food and a place to rest.

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event, usually lasting several days, where a large number of developers gather to work together on various computer programming projects.




Who are the judges?

Judges will be a panel that includes academics, designers and developers. We are currently in the process of determining judges. An official notice will be given at a later date.

What should I bring?

A laptop and a charger. It’s also a good idea to bring a sleeping bag/blanket/pillow. We will take care of the rest.

Are teenagers allowed to participate?

Unfortunately, due to insurance issues, participation is limited to ages 18 and above.
